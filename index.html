House Rules

Objective

Beat the dealer by having a hand total closer to 21 without going over.

Card Values

  • Number cards = their face value.
  • Face cards (J, Q, K) = 10.
  • Aces = 1 or 11 (whichever benefits your hand).

Gameplay

  • Place your bet before each round.
  • You and the dealer each receive 2 cards.
  • One of the dealer's cards remains face-down until their turn.

Player Actions

  • Hit: Take another card.
  • Stand: Keep your current hand.

Dealer's Turn

Dealer must hit until their total is 17 or higher.

Winning & Payouts

  • Blackjack (21 with 2 cards): You win 3x your bet (your bet + 2x bonus).
  • Regular Win: You win 2x your bet (your bet + 1x bonus).
  • Push/Draw: You get your bet back (1x your bet).
  • Loss: You lose your bet.

How to Win

  • Get a hand total closer to 21 than the dealer's without going over.
  • Get 21 with your first 2 cards (Blackjack) for the highest payout.
  • Win when the dealer busts (goes over 21).

Bust Rule

If you go over 21, you lose immediately regardless of the dealer's hand.
