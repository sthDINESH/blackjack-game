Blackjack Showdown

Welcome to Blackjack Showdown!

Step up to the table, place your bets, and let's see if Lady Luck's in your corner tonight!

Rules

Objective

Beat the dealer by having a hand total closer to 21 without going over.

Card Values

  • Number cards = their face value.
  • Face cards (J, Q, K) = 10.
  • Aces = 1 or 11 (whichever benefits your hand).

Gameplay

  • Place your bet before each round.
  • You and the dealer each receive 2 cards.
  • One of the dealer’s cards remains face-down until their turn.

Player Actions

  • Hit: Take another card.
  • Stand: Keep your current hand.

Dealer’s Turn

Dealer must hit until their total is 17 or higher.

Winning

  • If your hand is closer to 21 than the dealer’s, you win.
  • If the dealer busts (goes over 21), you win.
  • If both hands are equal, it’s a push (tie).

Bust Rule

If you go over 21, you lose immediately.
